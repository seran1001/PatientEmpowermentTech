About Us

Patient education is essential to ensure optimal outcomes for those who are transitioning to home care after vascular procedures. We sought to assess the efficacy of a novel mobile technology-based educational tool to increase patient knowledge, ease the transition to home care, and improve the health care experience. We provide a responsive Education system with a creative user interface and a medical graphic simulation for the patients' understanding of the process of their diagnosis and treatment
